Sign up for SMS alerts



Regular SMS Alert System:

  • Sign up to receive text-message alerts to any device with a valid phone number. These alerts will be sent to you whenever a bad batch of opioid drugs are potentially in your area.
  • To sign up, simply text JOIN to 000-000-0000. Reply with DONE at any time to stop alerts.





Geographic SMS Alert System:

  • Sign up to receive a Google Maps image sent to your cell phone showing exactly where an incident relating to bad opioids was reported along with the address of the location.
  • To sign up, text JOIN to 000-000-0000. Reply with DONE at any time to stop alerts.
